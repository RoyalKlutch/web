Frequently Asked Questions

How would i connect to the server? To connect to our FiveM server all you need to do is head into FiveM, press F8 and type "connect 161.97.140.226"

How would i join a department? To join a department you would need to head over to the "Applications" tab, then find the department you would like to apply for, then fill out the application

How do i apply for staff? To apply for staff you would need to head over to the "Applications" tab, then find the "Staff Application", then fill it out

What are the servers rules? All our rules can be found in the link that is tied to the title of this faq, if you have any question about our rules please join our discord
